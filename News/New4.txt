Marmara Restaurant home delivery now available throughout Bilkent with Yemek Sepeti
Lisa Baertlein
1
1.28
Marmara Restaurant fans across Bilkent can now get their favourite food delivered straight to their door.

The restaurant has partnered with online takeaway service Yemek Sepeti to offer deliveries through its app.

“We're thrilled to welcome Marmara Restaurant to the Yemek Sepeti platform, offering our customers in Bilkent an even wider choice of food than ever before,
“ Melih Akan, managing director at Yemek Sepeti, said.
